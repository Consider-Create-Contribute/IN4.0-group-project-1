About Capstone

Capstone Architecture prides itself on designing custom modern homes for contemporary living. We believe that successful design is built on supportive and honest partnerships with our clients We approach each of our designs with site-specific ideas and client-driven inspiration, working closely together to create elegent solutions for modern homes. We are committed to efficient and smart designs by preserving natural conditions as much as possible and collaborating with local craftsmen.

We love what we do and are excited to share our passion for design with our clients.

Our environmental commitment

Capstone has over 20 years of experience in this area. We were environmentally friendly before it was cool!

We view our environmental responsibilities very seriously, and any design consultation will take into account both local and global environmental issues. But if you would like a specific consultation in this area - either to improve on preexisting designs or to create your own eco paradise - we provide the expert services you need. In the long run, this can even save money through reduced heating costs and even electricity generation!
